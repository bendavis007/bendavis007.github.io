Davis receives his Doctorate of Philosophy

sentineltimes.com · May 7th, 2015 · 0 Comment
sentineltimes.com | Sentinel Times

Ben Davis just finished his Doctorate and is graduating on May, 8th from University of Arkansas, with a Doctorate of Philosophy in Space and Planetary Sciences
He has been doing research on the Spiral Arm of the Black Hole and got his Doctorate on the Logarithmic Spiral Arm Pitch Angle of Spiral Galaxies: Measurement and Relationship to Galactic Structure and Nuclear Supermassive Black Hole Mass.
